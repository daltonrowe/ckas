Get Involved! Join CKAS

By joining the National Audubon Society, you become a member of a group of people who are enthusiastic and committed to helping birds, caring for other local wildlife, and preserving your community. One year membership is only $20.

Memberships also make great gifts for friends and family!

As a member of the Audubon Society, you get...

  • A one-year subscription to Audubon magazine, an award-winning publication that features beautiful photography and thoughtful, conservation-oriented journalism.
  • Free admission to most of our National Audubon Centers. Enjoy the beauties at over 100 sites across the nation!
  • Membership in the National Audubon Society includes a free membership in the Central Kentucky Audubon Society, and opportunities to participate in Chapter activities like nature outings and educational programs. You will also receive the monthly Central Kentucky Audubon Chapter e-newsletter, filled with plenty of cool bird stuff happening in and around central Kentucky.

To become a member of National Audubon Society and your local Chapter, follow this link. When asked for your Chapter Identification Code, please select J51 from the drop-down menu.
